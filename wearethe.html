Together, We Are the 3.5%

The 3.5% Rule: Powerful Change Is Possible

History shows that when just 3.5% of a country's population joins in sustained, nonviolent resistance, it can lead to real political change. In the U.S., that’s only about 11.7 million people.

That might be enough to:

  • Break MAGA and GOP unity
  • Pressure resignations or impeachment
  • Defend democracy from authoritarian threats

Why We Don’t Need Everyone

We don’t need everyone protesting. What we need is enough people doing things that apply smart pressure — consistently and publicly. Here's how:

  1. Shift Public Opinion
    Politicians fear losing votes. If just 20–30% of Republican voters speak out, we create cracks in MAGA unity. Veterans, faith leaders, and business owners speaking up has a ripple effect.
  2. Target Leverage Points
    Focus on key swing states (PA, WI, GA, AZ, NV, NC), GOP donors, and vulnerable districts.
  3. Make Resistance Visible
    Protests, art, letters, phone calls, op-eds, and organizing all show the public: we are not alone.

Who Do We Need?

  • Everyday Americans willing to speak up
  • Parents, veterans, artists, students, workers
  • Local leaders who inspire others
  • You

What Can You Do?

  • Join or start a local democracy group
  • Use your platform — online or offline
  • Show up to protests and town halls
  • Talk to friends in swing states
